Hi, I am Yun Xiao

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics in the Department of Economics, University of Amsterdam (UvA). My supervisors are Hessel Oosterbeek and Pauline Rossi.

I am an applied microeconomist with interests in development and family economics. In my job market paper, titled 'Multidimensional Quality-Quantity Trade-off and Intergenerational Mobility: Evidence from China', I study how population policies affect fertility and different dimensions of child quality in rural China, and how the policies contribute to declining intergenerational mobility.

I am available for interviews during the American (ASSA) and European Job Market (EJME) meetings of the 2021/2022 job market.

Placement director Tinbergen Institute: Prof. Dr. Eric Bartelsman (e.j.bartelsman@tinbergen.nl)
